Site title: Piriapolis Chamber of Commerce.

Site pourpose.

The purpose of this site is to provide a community focused environment for promote the business-business opportunities for cooperation and the shared efforts for giving the locals and the tourist an attractive offer. Also to help the local business owners to get marketing and administration capacitation by sponsored courses and talks given by experts in the area. Together, the local entrepreneurs can dialogue with the government authorities and propose agreed changes for improving the economy and commerce in the area.
